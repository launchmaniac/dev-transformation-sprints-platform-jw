# Transformation Sprints Platform

**Mission:** Help people master practical life skills in focused 30-day sprints using proven frameworks

**Date Started:** November 20, 2025

---

## Platform Overview

**The Problem:**
People want to improve (parenting, relationships, productivity) but books and courses are overwhelming with no clear path or accountability.

**The Solution:**
- **30-day focused sprints** - digestible, achievable timeframe
- **Proven frameworks** - evidence-based methodology (Love & Logic, Whole-Brain Child, etc.)
- **Daily action steps** - tell people exactly what to do each day
- **Built-in accountability** - track progress, see results

## First Sprint: "Better Parenting in 30 Days"

**Target Audience:** Parents of kids ages 3-10 feeling overwhelmed/reactive

**Frameworks Used:**
- Love and Logic (practical application)
- Whole-Brain Child (understanding the "why")

**Core Pain Point:**
"I don't have the emotional bandwidth to deal with my kid right now"

**Transformation Promise:**
"Learn how to stay calm even when you're exhausted - in 30 days"
